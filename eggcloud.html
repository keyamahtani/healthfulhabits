Eggs On A Cloud:

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper

Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°F and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  • Separate egg yolks from whites and keep each yolk individually aside.
  • Combine egg whites and salt and whip until stiff peaks form.
  • Form 2 clouds on baking sheet at least 2 inches apart. Make a small indentation in the center.
  • Bake for 3-5 minutes or until egg whites are barely golden.
  • Add egg yolks to indentations and bake another 3-5 minutes.
  • Garnish with black pepper.
