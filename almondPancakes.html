Almond Flour Pancakes:

Makes 4 medium pancakes


Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/3 Tbsp vanilla extract

  • Honey, maple syrup, or berries for toppings (optional)

Instructions:

  • Mix all of the ingredients together until smooth and well-combined.
  • On a well-greased pan, pour the batter. Flip when golden.
  • Serve with drizzle of honey or maple syrup and berries.
