Green Smoothie Base:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup kale
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1/4 cup chopped pineapples
  • 1/4 cup mango chunks
  • Almond milk (quantity as desired)
  • Ice (quantity as desired)
  • 1 Tbsp nut butter

Pitaya Smoothie Base:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1/4 cup chopped pineapples
  • 1/4 cup mango chunks
  • 1/8 cup frozen pitaya
  • Almond milk (quantity as desired)
  • Ice (quantity as desired)
  • 1 Tbsp nut butter

  • Granola, Shredded Coconut, Berries, Chia Seeds, Honey (for toppings)

Instructions:

  • Blend all base ingredients together until smooth and thick.
  • Ensure that your base is not too liquidy.
  • As desired, add toppings to smoothie bowl.
