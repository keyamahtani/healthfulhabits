green smoothie base

ingredients:

if possible, use frozen fruit for each recipe.
  • 1/2 C. Kale
  • 1/2 Frozen Banana
  • 1/4 C. Chopped Pineapple
  • 1/4 C. Chopped Mango
  • Almond Milk (quantity as desired)
  • Ice (quantity as desired)
  • 1 Tbsp Nut Butter
  • Granola, Shredded Coconut, Berries, Honey (for toppings)

tropical smoothie base

ingredients:

  • 1/2 Frozen Banana
  • 1/4 C. Chopped Pineapple
  • 1/4 C. Chopped Mango
  • 1/4 C. Chopped Strawberries
  • Almond Milk (quantity as desired)
  • Ice (quantity as desired)
  • 1 Tbsp Nut Butter
  • Granola, Shredded Coconut, Berries, Honey (for toppings)

instructions:

  • Blend all base ingredients together until combined and thick.
  • As desired, add toppings.
  • Eat and enjoy!
