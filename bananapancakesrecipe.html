Banana Pancakes:

Makes 6-8 small pancakes


Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 dash of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder (optional)
  • Olive oil to grease pan

Instructions:

  • Mash the banana in a mixing bowl until smooth.
  • Stir in eggs, and beat well. Add cinnamon, chia seeds, and baking powder.
  • On a well-greased pan, pour the batter. Flip when golden.
