ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Ripe Banana
  • 1 Dash of Cinnamon
  • Olive Oil

instructions:

  • Mash the banana in a mixing bowl until it reaches a batter-like consistency.
  • Stir in eggs and cinnamon.
  • On a well greased pan, pour the batter in small pancake shapes. Flip when golden.
  • Eat and enjoy!
