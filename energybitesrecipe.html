Energy Bites


Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup ground cashew nuts
  • 2/3 almond butter
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 3/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats (May need more depending on consistency)

Instructions:

  • Blend all of the ingredients, except the oats, in a food processor until smooth
  • Stir in oats until a thick dough forms.
  • Roll the dough into bites and refrigerate.
