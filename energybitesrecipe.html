ingredients:

  • 2/3 C. Ground Cashew Nuts
  • 2/3 Nut Butter
  • 1/3 C. Maple Syrup OR Honey
  • 1 Tbsp. Chia Seeds
  • 3/4 C. Almond Milk
  • 1/2 C. Dark Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Tbsp Nut Butter
  • Rolled Oats (Quantity varies)

instructions:

  • Blend all of the ingredients together.
  • Add in oats until a thick dough forms.
  • Roll the dough into ball shapes. Refrigerate.
  • Eat and enjoy!
