ingredients:

  • 1/3 C. Rolled Oats
  • 1 tsp. Nut Butter or Applesauce
  • 1/2 C. Almond Milk or Orange Juice
  • 1 tsp. Chia Seeds
  • Fruit for garnishing

instructions:

  • Stir all of the ingredients together in a bowl or cup.
  • Refrigerate overnight.
  • Stir and add fruit on top.
  • Eat and enjoy!
