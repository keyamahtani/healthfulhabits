Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp nut butter or applesauce
  • 1/2 cup almond milk, orange juice, or cold brew
  • 1 tsp chia seeds
  • Fruit for garnishing

Instructions:

  • Stir all of the ingredients together in a bowl or cup.
  • Refrigerate overnight.
  • Stir mixture in the morning, then add fruit on top.
